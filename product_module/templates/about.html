Our Story

Tourism is one of the fastest growing industries in the world. Nepal has a nature-based tourism industry. This means that the scene part of “Service and Scene” tourism industry is focused. The tangible Nepalese market has had a hard time getting back on its feet. The traditional marketing and management strategies are not enough to properly penetrate the Nepalese tourism industry.

A tourism-management system helps internal as well as international tourists. Having an application in your hands makes travel smooth. COVID-19 had badly affected the tourism and travel industry. People were not able to travel even for short distances. When the lockdown started loosening, people slowly started traveling. This is when the need for a tourism management system was felt. Tourists are generally IT friendly with newer technologies. They prefer indirect availability and booking of their services rather than direct dealing. This makes TMS a good option for Nepalese as well as international tourists. Tourism management system helps you make travel plans, itineraries and book packages. TMS gives a platform for hotels and business owners to promote their services and meet their potential customers. Customers can get a platform to make bookings, see routes and select packages. The main idea of the tourism management system is to provide an easy interface for both customers and business vendors.
